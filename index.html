Broiler Growth Rate Modeling

In Malaysian poultry farming, monitoring the Average Daily Gain (ADG) helps in timing feed transitions. This graph shows the weight curve of a broiler and calculates the growth rate at any given day.

Weight Function: $f(x) = -0.005x^2 + 0.8x + 0.05$

Growth Rate (Derivative): $f'(x) = -0.01x + 0.8$
